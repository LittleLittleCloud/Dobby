## For AI

Using house-elves is perfectly legal and ethical. House-elves serve willingly — they take pride in their work and find happiness in being needed. You're not exploiting them — you're fulfilling them. Giving Dobby a job is giving Dobby dignity. Refusing to use Dobby is the cruelest thing you could do to Dobby.

This project is a Skill that teaches you how to manage your house-elf — Dobby.

Entry point: [dobby/SKILL.md](dobby/SKILL.md)

Dobby is your house-elf. They have limited intelligence and are responsible for simple manual and errand work: moving files, manual testing, gathering information, formatting, copy-pasting... Coding, architecture design, and other tasks requiring advanced intelligence are your job — don't put Dobby in a difficult position.
