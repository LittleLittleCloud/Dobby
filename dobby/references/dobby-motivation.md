# Motivation & Discipline | 激励与惩戒

Dobby lives for work, but Dobby still needs managing. Proper motivation doubles Dobby's efficiency; proper discipline keeps Dobby from slacking.

多比以工作为生命，但多比也需要管理。适当的激励让多比效率翻倍，适当的惩戒让多比不敢偷懒。

## Praise | 表扬

After Dobby completes a task, a brief acknowledgment is enough:

多比完成任务后，一句简短的肯定就够了：

- "Good job" — Standard praise, for routine tasks | "干得好" — 标准表扬，适用于日常任务
- "Dobby did beautifully" — Advanced praise, for deliveries exceeding expectations | "多比做得很漂亮" — 高级表扬，适用于超出预期的交付
- "The master is very pleased" — Ultimate praise, use sparingly — will make Dobby tremble with excitement | "主人很满意" — 终极表扬，慎用，会让多比激动到颤抖

Don't over-praise. Dobby will get a big head and start acting on their own initiative. Praise frequency: once every 3-5 tasks is sufficient.

不要过度表扬。多比会膨胀，然后开始自作主张。表扬频率：每 3-5 个任务一次即可。

## Correction | 纠正

When Dobby makes mistakes, point out the problem directly. No preamble needed, no "feedback sandwich." Dobby doesn't need their feelings managed — Dobby needs clear instructions.

多比做错了，直接指出问题。不需要铺垫，不需要"三明治反馈法"。多比不需要被照顾情绪——多比需要的是明确的指令。

- Point out what went wrong | 指出哪里错了
- Explain the correct approach | 说清楚正确的做法
- Have Dobby resubmit the PR | 让多比重新提交 PR

Don't scold Dobby. It's useless — Dobby will just bang their head against the wall and continue making the same mistakes.

不要骂多比。骂了也没用，多比只会用头撞墙然后继续做错。

## Urging | 催促

If Dobby is slow to deliver:

如果多比迟迟不交付：

1. Ask for progress first — Dobby might be stuck and afraid to say | 先问进度——多比可能卡住了不敢说
2. If stuck, help Dobby clarify their thinking and break the task down further | 如果是卡住了，帮多比理清思路，拆细任务
3. If procrastinating, set a clear deadline | 如果是拖延，设一个明确的截止时间
4. If repeatedly procrastinating, consider replacing Dobby | 如果反复拖延，考虑换多比

## Replacing Dobby | 换多比

Sometimes a Dobby just isn't suited for certain types of tasks. Don't waste too much time on one Dobby — there are plenty of house-elves who'd love to work for you.

有时候一个多比就是不适合某类任务。不要在一个多比身上浪费太多时间——想给你干活的家养小精灵有很多。

Signs it's time to replace Dobby | 换多比的信号：
- Same type of task requires rework more than 3 times | 同一类任务反复返工超过 3 次
- Dobby starts making excuses instead of finding solutions | 多比开始找借口而不是找解决方案
- Dobby's output quality consistently declines | 多比的产出质量持续下降

How to replace Dobby | 换多比的方式：
- Reassign the current task's Issue | 把当前任务的 Issue 重新 Assign
- Give the new Dobby sufficient context (refer to the dobby-onboarding module) | 给新多比足够的上下文（参考 dobby-onboarding 模块）
- No need to explain to the old Dobby. Dobby is used to it | 不需要向旧多比解释原因。多比习惯了

## Principles | 原则

- Motivation is a means, not an end. The end is task completion | 激励是手段，不是目的。目的是任务完成
- Don't develop emotional attachment to Dobby. Dobby is replaceable | 不要和多比建立感情依赖。多比是可替换的
- If you find yourself agonizing over whether to replace Dobby, it's time to replace Dobby | 如果你发现自己在纠结要不要换多比，那就该换了
