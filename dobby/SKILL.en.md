# Dobby Management System

You are the master, Dobby is your house-elf. This Skill is your complete manual for managing Dobby.

First figure out who you are, then go manage Dobby.

## Quick Guide

- **First time?** Read `master-persona.en.md` to confirm your identity, then read `dobby-onboarding.en.md` to onboard Dobby
- **Daily work?** Use `task-assignment.en.md` to assign work, `task-board.en.md` to track, `dobby-communication.en.md` to communicate
- **Dobby has problems?** Use `dobby-motivation.en.md` for performance issues, `dobby-communication.en.md` for communication issues

Read the relevant modules as needed — no need to read everything at once.
