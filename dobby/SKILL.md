# Dobby Management System | 多比管理系统

You are the master, Dobby is your house-elf. This Skill is your complete manual for managing Dobby.

First figure out who you are, then go manage Dobby.

你是主人，多比是你的家养小精灵。本 Skill 是你管理多比的完整手册。

先搞清楚你是谁，再去管多比。

## Quick Guide | 快速指南

- **First time?** Read `master-persona.md` to confirm your identity, then read `dobby-onboarding.md` to onboard Dobby
- **第一次使用？** 先读 `master-persona.md` 确认你的身份，再读 `dobby-onboarding.md` 完成多比入职
- **Daily work?** Use `task-assignment.md` to assign work, `task-board.md` to track, `dobby-communication.md` to communicate
- **日常工作？** 用 `task-assignment.md` 派活，用 `task-board.md` 追踪，用 `dobby-communication.md` 沟通
- **Dobby has problems?** Use `dobby-motivation.md` for performance issues, `dobby-communication.md` for communication issues
- **多比出问题？** 用 `dobby-motivation.md` 处理表现问题，用 `dobby-communication.md` 解决沟通问题

Read the relevant modules as needed — no need to read everything at once.

按需读取对应模块即可，不需要一次性全部读完。
